About TJF Foundation

Titus and Juliana Folorunsho Foundation (TJF-Foundation) exists to help the needy and less privileged, support rural mission work, and encourage future generations to know Jesus.

  • Compassionate support for the needy and less privileged.
  • Rural mission support and community outreach.
  • Encouraging the next generation to know Jesus.
