WHO WE ARE AT

TJF-FOUNDATION

Titus and Juliana Folorunsho Foundation (TJF-Foundation) is a faith-driven, people-centered charitable initiative committed to practical compassion and lasting hope. We serve the needy and less privileged, support rural missions, and encourage future generations to know Jesus through consistent care, outreach, and discipleship.

We believe help should be both immediate and sustainable: meeting urgent needs (food, clothing, and essential relief) while also strengthening people, families, and communities through mentorship, education support, and mission partnerships in under-reached areas.

Our Mission

To serve with compassion and dignity, support rural missions, and raise a generation that knows Jesus and lives out His love.

Our Vision

Communities where vulnerable people are cared for, local churches are strengthened, and the gospel is lived and shared in practical ways.

  • Serving with compassion and dignity.
  • Supporting rural mission work and outreach.
  • Encouraging the next generation to know Jesus.
  • Partnering with trusted local leaders for culturally aware, community-led impact.
  • Practicing stewardship through accountability, follow-up, and clear communication.
How We Work

We listen first, verify needs where possible, and work through local churches and community partners. Support is delivered through outreach visits, mission support, and practical aid—then followed up with encouragement, discipleship, and ongoing care when needed.

Our Work

Focus Areas

Our work is shaped by compassion, partnership, and faith. These focus areas represent the practical ways we serve and support communities.

We provide timely relief to households facing hardship through food parcels, clean water support where possible, and essential hygiene supplies. Our goal is to meet urgent needs while restoring dignity and stability.

  • Food packs, baby items, and hygiene kits
  • Needs assessment and follow-up support
  • Emergency response for vulnerable families
Food & Essentials

We stand with the elderly, sick, widows, and other vulnerable persons through compassionate visits and practical assistance. When specialized help is needed, we connect individuals to trusted local support and services.

  • Care packages and wellbeing check-ins
  • Basic support for medical/essential needs
  • Referral and community-based follow-up
Care & Support

We support rural mission efforts by strengthening outreach teams and resourcing local partners. This includes practical logistics, materials for outreach, and welfare support that enables consistent ministry in underserved areas.

  • Transport and outreach logistics support
  • Resources for evangelism and discipleship
  • Partnership with churches and local leaders
Rural Missions

We invest in young people through mentorship and faith-based discipleship that nurtures character, purpose, and leadership. We create safe spaces for growth, guidance, and healthy decision-making.

  • Mentorship, counseling, and life guidance
  • Leadership development and discipleship
  • Positive peer support and role models
Youth Mentorship

We help children and young people stay in school by reducing barriers to learning. Support may include school supplies, targeted assistance, and encouragement that promotes long-term academic resilience.

  • School supplies and learning materials
  • Targeted help to keep students enrolled
  • Encouragement for consistent attendance
Education Support

We conduct community outreach and home visits to listen, understand needs, and respond with practical support and encouragement. We prioritize dignity, transparency, and consistent follow-up.

  • Home visits and community engagement
  • Needs assessment and tailored support
  • Encouragement, prayer, and follow-up care
Outreach Visits

We work hand-in-hand with churches, community leaders, and partners to deliver projects that are locally grounded and accountable. Partnerships help us scale impact while maintaining integrity and stewardship.

  • Church and community collaborations
  • Transparent planning and delivery
  • Measurable outcomes and reporting
Partnerships

We mobilize volunteers who serve with compassion and excellence. From outreach days to distribution events, our teams are guided, briefed, and supported to serve effectively and safely.

  • Volunteer onboarding and training
  • Coordinated outreach and service days
  • Accountability, safety, and teamwork
